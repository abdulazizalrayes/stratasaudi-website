The Technical Partner Law Firms Are Missing

Legal teams win cases with evidence. But in construction disputes, the most decisive evidence is technical — buried in engineering details that lawyers are not trained to interpret.

Strata works alongside international and local law firms as their technical counterpart: identifying engineering details that strengthen or undermine a case, interpreting drawings, specifications, and site records, reconstructing what actually happened on the ground, and translating complex technical realities into language that supports legal strategy.

We do not replace legal counsel. We give them the technical ammunition they would not otherwise have.

Market Insight

Why Foreign Contractors
Misjudge Saudi Risk Exposure

Underestimation of procedural notice requirements that, when missed, forfeit substantial entitlements regardless of merit.

Overreliance on international legal interpretation without understanding local enforcement context and institutional behaviour.

Engineering documentation gaps that become critical weaknesses once disputes move beyond negotiation.

Misalignment between contract language and on-site realities, creating exposure that compounds over project lifecycle.

Reactive rather than preventive dispute strategy, leading to substantially higher cost of resolution.
