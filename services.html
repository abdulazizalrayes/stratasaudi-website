Our Services

Comprehensive contract risk management, FIDIC claims expertise, and technical advisory services for complex engineering projects across Saudi Arabia and internationally.

01

Pre-Contract Risk Review

Technical and commercial risk assessment of Saudi-governed contracts prior to execution. Our comprehensive review identifies contractual exposure before commitment, ensuring alignment with project objectives and organizational risk tolerance.

Key Deliverables

  • Risk allocation structure analysis
  • Variation clause exposure review
  • Notice provision compliance mapping
  • Payment mechanism stress testing

Who It's For: Project owners, contractors, and developers executing contracts under Saudi law or FIDIC-based frameworks seeking to identify commercial and technical risks before signing.

02

Project Risk Oversight

Ongoing embedded advisory during project execution phases. We provide continuous monitoring and documentation to manage contractual compliance, detect emerging risks early, and support sound decision-making throughout the project lifecycle.

Key Deliverables

  • Real-time contractual compliance monitoring
  • Monthly risk register maintenance
  • Delay and disruption early detection
  • Change order evaluation and documentation

Who It's For: Project teams, contractors, and facility managers requiring continuous risk oversight, documentation support, and early warning systems for contractual and schedule issues.

03

Pre-Litigation Technical Advisory

Expert-level construction dispute support before formal proceedings. We provide rigorous technical analysis and documentation to substantiate claims, evaluate defenses, and support negotiated resolution. Our advisory bridges engineering expertise and dispute preparation.

Key Deliverables

  • Delay analysis (as-planned vs as-built, collapsed as-built, time impact)
  • Quantum calculation and documentation packaging
  • Witness statement preparation
  • Dispute avoidance negotiation support

Who It's For: Contractors, subcontractors, and project owners with emerging disputes seeking to prepare claims, evaluate exposures, and explore resolution pathways before litigation or arbitration.

04

Independent Technical Opinion

Third-party engineering expert assessments for arbitration or court proceedings. We provide impartial, defensible expert opinions backed by rigorous forensic analysis, program reconstruction, and causal modeling to support formal dispute resolution.

Key Deliverables

  • Programme forensics and CPM analysis
  • Causation opinions on delay and disruption
  • Concurrency evaluation
  • Loss and expense quantum modeling

Who It's For: Legal counsel, arbitrators, and parties in formal dispute proceedings requiring independent technical evidence, expert testimony, and engineering analysis for arbitration or litigation.

05

Supply Chain & Vendor Risk Assessment

Due diligence services for mega-project procurement. We conduct comprehensive vendor and subcontractor assessments to evaluate capability, contractual alignment, and performance risk, supporting informed procurement decisions on large capital projects.

Key Deliverables

  • Vendor capability and capacity auditing
  • Contractual flow-down compliance checks
  • Performance bond and guarantee adequacy review
  • Sub-contractor risk profiling

Who It's For: Project developers, main contractors, and procurement teams selecting critical vendors and subcontractors for large infrastructure and engineering projects.
